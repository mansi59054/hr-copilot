# IT Onboarding Manual (Global)

## 1. Accounts
New joiners receive SSO credentials on day 1. MFA setup is mandatory within 24 hours.

## 3. VPN Setup
Install the corporate VPN client from the self-service portal. As of Feb 2026, only client v5 is supported.
Remote workers MUST keep VPN active during work hours (see Global Security Policy 2.4).

## 5. Equipment
Standard issue: laptop with disk encryption. Personal devices are not permitted for company data.
