# Manager Handbook, Extract: Leave Approvals

- Approve/reject leave within 3 working days of request.
- UK: leave year runs Jan-Dec. No carry-over (see 2024 Handbook 4.1) EXCEPT where HR announces exceptions.
- India: carry-forward up to 10 days privilege leave (2025 Handbook 4.1).
- Escalate conflicts between regional policies to your HR Business Partner. Regional handbooks take precedence over this extract.
